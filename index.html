Our Mission & Vision

We are a small team of developers that created Prayer mode app to serve our Ummah worldwide. Prayer mode addresses a simple need; focusing on Salah without being disturbed by mobile calls and notifications.

Absolute Privacy

We believe that devotion and data should never mix. We commit to No Ads, No Tracking, and No Data Collection.

Community-Driven

We rely on user feedback, suggestions, and translators to constantly refine and expand the app, ensuring it serves the global Ummah effectively. This is our sadaqah jariyah (ongoing charity) to the Muslim community.

Transparency & Trust

We believe trust comes from transparency. As an Open Source project, our code is publicly available for anyone to review, guaranteeing there are no hidden malicious functions.
